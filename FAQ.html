Can you get covid multiple times?

Yes you can have covid multiple times, however after being infected with the virus your immune system will make it harder to attract it and it won't be as severe.

What are the symptoms?

Some of the common ones are fever, cough, sneezing, runny nose, loss of taste, among others. However not everyone will have symptoms.

What is the fatality rate?
